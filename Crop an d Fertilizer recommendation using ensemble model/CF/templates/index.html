About Crop & Fertilizer Recommendation

Learn how we help optimize your crop yield with the best fertilizer recommendations based on crop type and soil requirements.

Types of Crops

Crops can be divided into several categories such as cereals, pulses, fruits, vegetables, and oilseeds. Each crop requires specific conditions for growth and an optimal choice of fertilizer to maximize yield.

Types of Fertilizers

Fertilizers are crucial in providing essential nutrients for plant growth. Common types include nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium, and organic fertilizers. Understanding which fertilizer suits which crop is key to a bountiful harvest.

Best Practices

Using the right amount of fertilizer at the right time helps improve crop productivity. Over-fertilizing can harm the crop and the environment, while under-fertilizing can result in poor growth.
